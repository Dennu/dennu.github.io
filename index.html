Dangerous Goods Shipper's Declaration (DGD).

To ship dangerous goods by Air, consignors are required to prepare a form certifying that the cargo has been packed, labeled and declared according with the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR).

The International Maritime Dangerous Goods(IMDG).

An International guideline to the safe transportation or movement of dangerous goods or hazardous materials by water on vessel.
